Taxi Chaos

Taxi Chaos is a 3D linear open-world taxi simulator built in Unity, where you play as a driver navigating a low-poly city, picking up NPC passengers and dropping them off as quickly and safely as possible. I designed and implemented the entire game system from scratch, handling core mechanics, AI traffic, mission logic, and rewards.
